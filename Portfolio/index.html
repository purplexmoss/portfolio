A resource website for patients who who will be undergoing cataract surgery.

Cataract Resource Website: Using my industry knowledge and my experience in surgery counseling at an ophthalmology clinic, I've started to put together a website of resources for potential cataract surgery patients. The idea is to educate patients on cataracts and provide details about the most current intraocular lens implants on the market in order to aid them in making informed decisions about their vision.

404 Page Concept: Encountering a 404 page can be frustrating for a user. This is the first in a collection of 404 page concepts I am working on to help create a more user-friendly experience when an HTTP error does occur.
